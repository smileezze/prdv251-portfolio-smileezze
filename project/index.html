My Mission in Life

Coding creative side projects, crafting the perfect mocha as a barista, and chasing the ultimate chocolate bar—one pixel, one cocoa percentage, and one retro Game Boy at a time.

  • Obsessively curates a personal collection of single-origin dark chocolate bars, complete with detailed tasting notes on origins, flavor profiles, and ethical sourcing (he's currently hunting for the perfect 80%+ bar with bright fruit notes).
  • Restores and mods vintage Game Boys in his spare time, turning scratched relics into custom beauties with fresh screens, buttons, and even themed shells inspired by his favorite chocolate aesthetics.
  • Master intricate latte art at the coffee shop using cocoa powder stencils, often turning mochas into edible masterpieces like hearts, ferns, or tiny pixelated designs that surprise regular customers.
